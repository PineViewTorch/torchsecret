TorchSecret Image Search

For use by staff members to find images for postcards

Searches Google Images for items "labeled for reuse"

Important! Make sure that, on finding an image, you find two important details:

  1. The license itself - make sure it does, in fact, allow you to use the image. (On Flickr, for example, find the link on the right sidebar that says "some rights reserved".) We are a non-profit publication, so you may use images under a non-commerical license.
  2. The creator of the image - if the license requires attribution, which it likely will, this will have to be listed somewhere in the paper.

This information must be included on submitting your postcard!

If you just grab an image, lose the page, and then submit your secret, you will be forced to start from scratch, as we can not use any postcard that violates copyright, and attribution is a key part of most re-use licenses.

Questions? Ask them.
